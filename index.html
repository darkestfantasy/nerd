What can I ask We to do for me?

Anything that will help you with your education or business. As long as it's legal is not connected to facilitating drug use, gambling, cheating or violation of any uni policy. Anything at all... Try us out!

How fast will I get it delivered?

If it's a physical product you require - it will depend on the delivery time of a vendor such as Amazon. While if its something we can deliver over the phone or email - we can do it asap.

How much does it cost to use We111?

Its free to text us and request your service. We will charge a small premium over the lowest possible cost of the goods or services that you need. We will send you exact price and agree it with you BEFORE we charge you anything! So no worries, you will know the exact price and can accept or reject it.

How can I pay and is it secure?

You can pay with PayPal or any major Credit/Debit card online. We will send you a link to the invoice. This link will take you to the secure PayPal page where you can process your payment directly from your mobile phone. PayPal page where you enter your card data is a 128-bit encrypted HTTPS page that is hosted and secured by the largest online payment provider in the world - PayPal. We111 does not get any data about you or your card whatsoever.
